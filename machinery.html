Who can become a rental partner on AGRIVERSE?

Anyone who owns agricultural machinery, tools, or equipment — from individual farmers to local dealers and agri-service providers — can register and start renting through AGRIVERSE.

How does AGRIVERSE verify listed machines?

Once you register, our team inspects your machinery for condition, functionality, and safety compliance before approving your listing. Verified machines get the “AGRIVERSE Certified” badge.

What are the costs or commissions involved?

There are no upfront fees. AGRIVERSE charges a small commission only after a successful rental, ensuring full transparency and fair earnings.

AGRIVERSE provides coverage options and assists in resolving any damages or disputes through our verified renter policy and support team.

Absolutely! You can list as many verified machines as you own — each will have its own profile and rental schedule.
