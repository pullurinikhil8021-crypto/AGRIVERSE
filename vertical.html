What is Vertical Farming?

Vertical farming is a modern agricultural innovation where crops are grown in stacked layers ‚Äî often in controlled indoor environments. It combines hydroponics, aeroponics, and smart automation to maximize yield while minimizing space and water use.

With increasing urbanization and shrinking arable land, vertical farming provides a sustainable, high-yield alternative that can grow vegetables, fruits, and herbs year-round regardless of weather.
