Safe Use & Application Guidelines

Smart Protection Begins with Safe Practice

Proper use of fertilizers and pesticides not only ensures your crops but also protects your health, your crops, and the environment. Follow these simple yet essential safety measures to make every application efficient, responsible, and safe for everyone on your farm.

Know your product

Always read and follow the label instructions carefully before mixing or spraying.

Wear Protective Gear

Use gloves, masks, and goggles to prevent skin or inhalation exposure.

Weather

Avoid spraying during high wind or rain to prevent drift and wastage.

Use the Right Dosage

Use gloves, masks, and goggles to prevent skin or inhalation exposure.

Clean Equipment

Wash sprayers and containers thoroughly after each application.

Store Safely

Keep pesticides in their original containers, away from children, animals, and food.
